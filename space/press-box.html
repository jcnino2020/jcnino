URGENT 11-15-1996 18:42 GMT

LOONEY TUNES CHALLENGE MONSTARS TO BASKETBALL MATCH

Following an attempted extra-terrestrial kidnapping of Bugs Bunny and the Looney Tunes crew by the Monstars, Bugs Bunny has negotiated a high-stakes basketball challenge. The game will decide the freedom of the Looney Tunes. If the Monstars win, the Looney Tunes will be forced to become the premier attraction at Moron Mountain amusement park.

BROADCAST 11-14-1996 09:15 GMT

MICHAEL JORDAN AGREES TO COACH TUNE SQUAD

Global basketball phenomenon Michael Jordan has officially come out of retirement in Looney Tune Land to lead the Tune Squad as player-coach. Jordan commented, "They've stolen the talent of the NBA's best players, but we have Bugs, Lola, and Daffy. We're going to give it everything we've got."

RELEASE 11-12-1996 14:00 GMT

WARNER BROS. ANNOUNCES HISTORIC INTER-GALACTIC RELEASE

Warner Bros. Pictures has announced the release of Space Jam, a groundbreaking hybrid feature film blending live-action athletics with classic animation. Co-starring basketball legend Michael Jordan and the iconic Bugs Bunny, the film is scheduled to hit planet Earth theaters this November.
