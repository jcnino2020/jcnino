From Upstream Data To Downstream Safety.

A predictive river monitoring system connecting mountain communities in Murcia to coastal barangays in Bacolod. Know the flood is coming 2 hours before it hits.

Flutter Mobile App Firebase Backend Geolocation

Source-to-Sea Logging

Field observers in upland areas (Murcia/DSB) log rainfall intensity. Replaces reactive "Help me" buttons with proactive data entry.

Impact Projection

Algorithms map upstream surges to downstream rivers. We don't just tell you *where* the water is, we tell you *when* it arrives.

Targeted Alerts

Automatic notifications specifically for downstream barangays (Mandalagan, Banago) located along the critical river path.

The Mobile Interface

Designed for Low-Signal Areas

The mobile app is used by Bantay Bukid and Barangay Tanods. It caches data when offline and uploads automatically when a signal is found. No complex maps—just simple buttons for rapid reporting.
