Historical Precedents

Real-world incidents of system integer overflows

🎬

YouTube & Gangnam Style

In 2014, Psy's "Gangnam Style" video exceeded 2,147,483,647 views. YouTube's views counter at the time used a signed 32-bit integer, causing it to break and show negative numbers.

IMPACT: Backend update to 64-bit views counter
🚀

NASA Deep Impact

The space probe's internal timer tracked time in tenths of a second since launch. In 2013, this counter overflowed a signed 32-bit limit, launching a reboot loop that severed link signals.

IMPACT: Total loss of communication with the spacecraft
✈️

Boeing 787 Dreamliner

An FAA airworthiness directive reported that the generator control unit (GCU) software counter overflowed a 32-bit register after exactly 248 days of continuous operation, shutting down electrical generators.

IMPACT: FAA mandated powercycle interval of 248 days
