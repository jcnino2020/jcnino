Introduction

The Unix Millennium Bug

The Year 2038 problem (Y2K38) relates to the representing of time in many digital systems. Many legacy databases and OS kernels count time as seconds since the Unix Epoch (Jan 1, 1970) using a signed 32-bit integer.

Explore our interactive dashboard tabs to simulate the crash, review historical precedents, and inspect the 64-bit architecture solutions.
